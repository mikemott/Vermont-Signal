<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Secondary accomplishments</h3>



<p>Hartford High School freshman <strong>Ezra Tane</strong> will represent Windsor County at the Vermont State Youth Council for the next three years. The council, which includes 28 members from around the state, gives advice to the governor and Legislature about topics that affect young people in the state.</p>



<p>Lebanon High School&#8217;s <strong>French Honor Society</strong> chapter has been named a Bronze Level sponsor by the American Association of Teachers of French.</p>
